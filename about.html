Melissa Wood is the gal behind melimba.
What does "melimba" mean? No idea. The idea came to her one night in college... it sounds kind of like her name, Melissa, but not.
Viola! melimba was born.

Melissa earned her Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Painting/Drawing at Brigham Young University. After college, she moved with her husband to Texas and has been living there since. After 6 years in Texas, she feels like she should be able to wear cowboy boots and be taken seriously. Also, she likes to use the word "y'all" in her every day jargon. Melissa, Aaron, and her two (almost three) children enjoy hanging out, playing games, watching movies, and going on adventures.

Her younger sister, Becca, moved to Texas for a fancy pants advertising company. Luckily, she also graduated from Brigham Young University but in Graphic Design. Score. She helped with some of the fabric lines, (under the alias of "Beccabury"), gives Melissa honest opinions about design projects, and actually was the one who built this website. Melissa is really glad to have Becca in her life for SEVERAL reasons.

If you are interested in having us create your next project, feel free to email your ideas. We can work you up a quote and help you make your vision a success! Feel free to pull up a chair, take a look around, and enjoy.

Thanks for stopping by. We're glad you came.
